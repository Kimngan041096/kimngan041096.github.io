Favorite Music

I enjoy music that has depth and emotion, the kind that really makes you reflect. Whether it’s through soulful lyrics or intricate melodies, I find myself drawn to songs that speak to my heart. Below are my top 3:

Million Years Ago
By Adele – A soulful ballad that reflects on nostalgia and the passage of time. Its heartfelt lyrics, combined with her powerful vocals, evoke deep emotion and a sense of introspection.
Someone Like You
By Adele – A poignant ballad about heartbreak and unrequited love.
APT
By Bruno Mars and Rosé (BlackPink) – A beautiful and catchy melody with smooth, soulful vocals that blend perfectly.
